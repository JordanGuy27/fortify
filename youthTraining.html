Youth Training (ages 9-14)

Our youth program is designed to build a strong foundation of movement and develop athleticism for those new to training. This age group is critical for motor skill development and learning safe and efficient movement patterns that will set the ground work for speed and force development, as well as, reduce the chance of injuries, in the future. Athletes will be introduced to functional exercises in an engaging, fun, and challenging training environment.

Program Focus

  • 6 Foundations of Movement
  • Stability
  • Co-ordination
  • Proprioception
  • Conditioning

*60 minute training sessions
